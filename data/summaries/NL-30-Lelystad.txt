Occupancy at 1BOX Lelystad has climbed steadily over the three-month window, rising 1.8 percentage points on a square-meter basis (from 82.4% to 84.2%) and 1.0 point on a unit basis (from 79.8% to 80.8%), though rental velocity has weakened significantly—net additions fell from 13 units in March to just 3 in May, driven by declining move-ins. Revenue increased 5% across the span to €53,233, and the average rate per square meter recovered to €16.28 in May after dipping in April. Arrears outstanding improved notably from €7,936 in April to €5,889 in May, though the arrears case count remains elevated at 53 accounts.
