Occupancy has strengthened since January, rising 4.7 percentage points on a square-meter basis from 78.7% to 82.4% in March, driven by accelerating rental velocity with net additions of 13 units in March alone after modest gains in February. Revenue has remained stable around 50,700 despite slight downward pressure on average rate per square meter (down 0.33 per sqm from January to March), indicating occupancy gains are offsetting rate softness. Arrears spiked sharply in February to over 51,000 but have since normalized to 7,682 in March, suggesting a collection issue was resolved.
