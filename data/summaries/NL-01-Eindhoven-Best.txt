Occupancy has grown steadily since January, with square-metre occupancy rising from 88.2% to 90.9%, while unit occupancy held stable at 86.4% through February and March. Rental velocity weakened considerably, shifting from a net loss of 9 units in January to near-flat performance in February and March, indicating the site has stabilized around 261 rented units rather than regaining lost capacity. Revenue per square metre improved slightly from 20.55 to 21.23 despite flat rental activity, driving overall net revenue up 4.8% across the period. Arrears spiked sharply in February to 36,497.51 but recovered dramatically by March to 1,131.40, suggesting a collection or write-off event rather than a persistent payment issue.
