Occupancy declined sharply in June, falling from 85.0% to 80.7% on a unit basis after a modest recovery in May, with net losses of 7 units in April and 14 units in June offsetting a brief 2-unit gain in May. Revenue contracted 4.4% over the three-month span, from €32,576 in April to €31,150 in June, while average rate per square meter remained relatively stable around €22.12–€22.43. The site experienced elevated vacates in June (22 units) compared to prior months, though this was partially offset by consistent move-ins of 7 units monthly. Arrears showed volatility, spiking to €3,048 in May before improving to €1,387 in June, suggesting collection efforts have tightened despite the occupancy pressure.
