Occupancy by unit count improved meaningfully in May, rising from 75.4% in March to 77.7%, driven by a significant acceleration in net rentals to +15 units compared to just +2 units in each of the prior two months. Revenue remained relatively flat across the three-month span (€69,738 to €69,647), with per-sqm rates holding steady around €14.75–€14.79. Arrears have deteriorated notably, nearly quadrupling from €3,308 in March to €13,756 by May, warranting attention despite stable occupancy and rental velocity trends.
