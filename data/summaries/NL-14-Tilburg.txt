Occupancy at 1BOX Tilburg peaked in May at 77.7% but has since declined to 76.0% in June, erasing the modest gains made earlier in the quarter. Rental velocity turned negative in June with net vacates of 11 units following a strong May that added 15 units, driven by a sharp rise in move-outs (31 units) against only 20 move-ins. Revenue remained relatively flat across the three-month span at approximately €69,000 monthly, with rent-per-sqm stable around €14.75–€14.79. Arrears have escalated significantly, climbing from €5,993 in April to €18,486 by June, suggesting growing collection pressure despite a slight decrease in the count of delinquent accounts.
