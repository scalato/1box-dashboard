1BOX Tilburg experienced a sharp occupancy dip in February, falling 1.8 percentage points on a per-unit basis as move-ins slowed to 15 while vacates accelerated to 27, resulting in a net loss of 12 units. March showed recovery with net rentals of 2 units and occupancy stabilizing near 75% on both metrics, though absolute occupancy remains below January's 76.8%. Revenue per square meter improved steadily from 14.15 in January to 14.77 in March despite flat occupancy, indicating successful rate management. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to over 64,000 outstanding but dropped sharply in March to just 3,308, suggesting either strong collection efforts or write-offs of delinquent accounts in that month.
