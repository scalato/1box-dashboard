1BOX Heerlen has experienced a sharp decline in occupancy since January, dropping from 82.5% unit occupancy to 78.7% in March, driven primarily by a severe net loss of nine units in March after modest gains in the prior two months. Rental velocity weakened significantly in March with only three move-ins against 12 vacates, reversing the stronger activity seen in February. Revenue has remained relatively flat despite the occupancy drop, hovering near 18,000 monthly, though rent-per-sqm edged up from 13.88 to 14.39. Arrears spiked sharply in February to over 16,000 before recovering to 436 in March, suggesting a collection issue that was largely resolved.
