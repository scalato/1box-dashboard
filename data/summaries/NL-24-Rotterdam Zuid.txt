1BOX Rotterdam Zuid showed mixed momentum over the three-month period, with occupancy rising slightly from 86.2% to 86.5% in April before declining to 85.3% by May, a net loss of 0.9 percentage points across the span. Rental velocity turned negative in May with a net decline of 8 units after a modest gain of 3 units in April, driven by elevated vacate activity (21 move-outs versus 14 move-ins). Revenue remained relatively stable, growing from €81,691 to €84,191 despite occupancy softening, supported by a rate increase to €23.41 per sqm by May. Arrears is a concern, rising sharply to 108 accounts outstanding (€23,272 total) by May, representing a significant deterioration from 45 accounts in March.
