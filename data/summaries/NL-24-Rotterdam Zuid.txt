1BOX Rotterdam Zuid showed modest occupancy gains since January, with unit occupancy climbing from 84.7% to 86.2% despite flat net rentals in March after a strong February rebound of nine units. Square-meter occupancy rose 1.9 points to 89.2% over the quarter, though revenue declined slightly from €82,345 to €81,691 as average rent per square meter fell from €23.44 to €23.14. Arrears spiked sharply in February to €79,841 across 106 accounts but have since recovered to €21,948 across 45 accounts, suggesting collection efforts were effective in March.
