Occupancy at 1BOX Six-Fours peaked in February at 86.3% unit occupancy before declining to 84.0% in March, down 3.1 points from January. Rental velocity turned negative in March with net losses of 8 units, reversing strong February momentum of net plus 13 units. Revenue remained relatively stable across the quarter at approximately 48,000–49,600 monthly despite occupancy fluctuations, with rates per square meter edging up 1.6% from January to March. Arrears outstanding bounced back to 59,713 in March after improving in February, with account delinquency rising from 19 accounts in February to 24 in March.
