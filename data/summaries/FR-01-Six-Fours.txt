Occupancy at 1BOX Six-Fours declined steadily over the three-month span, dropping 1.8 percentage points from 85.6% unit occupancy in April to 84.2% in June, driven by negative net rentals of -2 units in June after flat activity in May. Revenue per square meter improved consistently, rising from €20.87 to €22.28, yet total net revenue growth stalled as the occupancy loss offset rate gains. Arrears remain elevated at €62,624 across 21 accounts as of June, having spiked in May before a modest improvement, warranting closer collection attention.
