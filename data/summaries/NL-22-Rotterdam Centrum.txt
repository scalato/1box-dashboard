Occupancy at 1BOX Rotterdam Centrum has declined 0.4 percentage points on a unit basis since March, falling from 71.7% to 71.1%, with May showing particular weakness as the site lost net 10 units despite modest move-in activity of 18 units against 28 vacates. Rental velocity turned negative in May after a modest net gain of 5 units in April, signaling weakening demand or increased turnover. Revenue has remained relatively flat at approximately €89,000–90,000 monthly, while average rent per square meter slipped from €26.04 to €25.84 over the period. Arrears spiked sharply in April to €22,738 before improving in May to €14,127, though arrears count remains elevated at 100 accounts.
