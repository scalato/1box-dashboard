Occupancy at 1BOX Rotterdam Centrum declined from 80.2% to 78.0% between January and February on a sqm basis before recovering slightly to 79.5% in March, though unit-level occupancy has remained relatively flat around 71–73%. Rental velocity has been weak, with net losses of 14 units in February followed by near-flat performance in March (net +2), indicating struggled leasing momentum over the three-month span. Revenue has softened, declining from €91,708 in January to approximately €89,900 in both February and March, while rate per sqm has edged down from €26.37 to €26.04. Arrears spiked sharply in February to €77,621 (113 accounts) before dropping back to €12,631 in March, suggesting collection issues in mid-period that have since improved, though arrears count remains elevated at 117 accounts.
