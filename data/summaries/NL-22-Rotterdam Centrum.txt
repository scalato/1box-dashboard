1BOX Rotterdam Centrum has experienced declining occupancy over the three-month period, with occupancy by square meter falling from 80.0% in April to 78.9% in June, while unit occupancy dropped from 72.2% to 71.1%. Rental velocity deteriorated sharply in May with net negative 10 units, though the site stabilized in June with zero net rentals as move-ins and move-outs balanced at 28 each. Revenue declined modestly from €89,624 in April to €88,643 in June, with rates per square meter remaining stable around €25.80. Arrears outstanding improved significantly from €22,739 in April to €15,282 in June, with the arrears account count dropping from 118 to 58.
