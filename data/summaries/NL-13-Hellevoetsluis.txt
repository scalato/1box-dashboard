Occupancy at 1BOX Hellevoetsluis declined sharply in February to 85.3% (down 2.2 points from January) before rebounding strongly in March to 89.7%, driven by a significant improvement in rental velocity with net +15 units in March compared to net -6 units in both January and February. Revenue remained relatively flat across the three months (€41,900–€43,260), though rent-per-sqm edged down slightly to €23.92 in March. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to €35,894 across 29 accounts before normalizing in March to €3,482, suggesting a collection issue was resolved.
