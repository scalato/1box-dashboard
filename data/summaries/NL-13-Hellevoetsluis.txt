Occupancy at 1BOX Hellevoetsluis has declined sharply over the three-month window, dropping from 89.7% unit occupancy in April to 84.1% in June, with square-meter occupancy falling 5.6 percentage points across the same span. Rental velocity has turned negative, with the site losing 6 units net in May and 10 units net in June, driven by elevated vacate activity (17 units vacated in both May and June versus only 7–13 move-ins). Revenue has softened in parallel, declining from 44,467 euros in May to 42,195 euros in June despite a slight uptick in per-sqm rates. Arrears count improved meaningfully, falling from 40 cases in May to 20 in June with total outstanding arrears at 2,972 euros, though this improvement is offset by the underlying occupancy pressure.
