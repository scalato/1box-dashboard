Occupancy declined notably in May, dropping from 89.7% to 86.9% on a unit basis as the site posted net negative rentals of six units—driven by 17 vacates against only 11 move-ins. Rental velocity weakened significantly across the period, with net rentals falling from a strong 15 units in March to just 1 unit in April before turning negative in May. Revenue surprisingly grew from €42,543 in March to €44,467 in May despite occupancy pressure, supported by a rate increase to €24.26 per sqm in May. Arrears have become a concern, climbing from €3,482 outstanding across 34 accounts in March to €4,923 across 40 accounts by May, signaling a potential collection issue alongside the occupancy weakness.
