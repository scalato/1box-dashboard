Occupancy by unit count rose from 70.3% in January to 73.5% in February—a gain of 9 rented units—then plateaued in March at 73.3% with minimal net movement. Net rentals peaked in February with 10 new units, but slowed significantly to just 1 unit in March as move-ins dropped from 17 to 9. Revenue remained flat across the period (around $29.6k–$30.1k monthly), while rent per square meter declined slightly from $17.28 to $17.12. Arrears spiked sharply in February to $23.2k before improving to $4.1k by March, suggesting a collection recovery rather than new delinquency.
