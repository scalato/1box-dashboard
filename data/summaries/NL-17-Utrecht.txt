1BOX Utrecht showed steady occupancy growth across the three-month period, rising from 66.4% to 71.0% by unit count and 71.7% to 75.8% by square meter, driven by consistent net rentals of 14–20 units per month. Revenue climbed 9% from March to May (€37,003 to €40,342), with rate-per-sqm recovering to €12.96 in May after dipping slightly in April. Arrears spiked to €6,534 across 58 accounts in April but improved by May, settling at €4,694 across 49 accounts, suggesting collections recovered following a mid-period disruption.
