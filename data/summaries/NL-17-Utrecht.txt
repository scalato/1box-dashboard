Occupancy at 1BOX Utrecht peaked in May at 71.0% unit occupancy before declining to 68.9% in June, erasing the prior month's gains and falling 1.0 point below April levels. Net rental activity turned negative in June with 9 units lost (18 move-ins versus 28 vacates), reversing the steady net gain of 14 units recorded in both April and May. Revenue held relatively stable across the quarter at approximately €39,000–€41,000 monthly despite occupancy volatility, driven by rate-per-sqm growth from €12.41 to €13.36. Arrears declined meaningfully from €6,533 in April to €4,694 in May but rebounded to €5,431 in June, indicating collection pressure may be returning.
