1BOX Utrecht shows strong momentum across the three-month period, with occupancy climbing from 61.3% to 66.4% by unit count and 65.4% to 71.7% by square meter, while net rentals accelerated significantly—from 4 units in January to 20 units in March. Revenue grew 7.5% from January to March (€34,444 to €37,003), though the rate per square meter dipped slightly to €12.53 in March despite higher overall occupancy. Arrears spiked unexpectedly in February to €30,822 before falling back to €4,133 in March, with occupant count stable at 42 across the latter two months.
