Occupancy at 1BOX Venlo bottomed in April at 71.7% unit occupancy before recovering to 73.5% in May, though still below the March baseline of 73.9%. The site shifted to positive rental momentum in May with net +4 units after two months of declines, driven by a sharp drop in vacates from 11 to 4 units. Revenue remained relatively flat across the three months (ranging €20,264 to €20,509), while per-sqm rates held steady near €18.50. Arrears have escalated significantly, rising from €513 in March to €4,525 in May—a nine-fold increase warranting attention despite stable tenant count.
