Occupancy has stabilized at 79.8% by unit count and 81.3% by square metre since February, after declining 0.5 percentage points in January; net rentals improved from negative 7 units in January to positive 1 unit in March, showing rental velocity has turned slightly positive. Revenue has declined modestly from €32,049 in January to €31,526 in March, driven by a drop in average rate per square metre from €21.66 to €21.33. Arrears spiked sharply to €26,947 in February but recovered to €2,655 in March, though arrears count remains elevated at 28 accounts compared to 20 in January, warranting continued monitoring.
