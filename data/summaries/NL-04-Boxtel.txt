Occupancy at 1BOX Boxtel has climbed steadily from 82.2% unit occupancy in January to 88.8% in March, a gain of 6.6 percentage points driven by strong net rentals of 7 units in February, though momentum slowed to just 1 net unit in March. Revenue has grown from €14,967 to €16,034 across the three-month span, outpacing the modest occupancy gains, while the rate per sqm has remained stable around €21.30. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to €15,452 before dropping back to €394 in March, suggesting a collections issue was resolved rather than an ongoing payment problem.
