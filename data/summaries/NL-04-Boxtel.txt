Occupancy has declined steadily from 88.8% in March to 82.1% in May, a drop of 6.7 percentage points driven by consistent negative rental velocity—the site lost 6 net units in April and 3 more in May despite modest move-in activity. Revenue has eroded correspondingly, falling from €16,034 in March to €15,542 in May, while rate-per-sqm has ticked up slightly to €22.42. Arrears are a growing concern, spiking from €394 across 6 accounts in March to €2,957 across 11 accounts in May, signaling emerging collection issues.
