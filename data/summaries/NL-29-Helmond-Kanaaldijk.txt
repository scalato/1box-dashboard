Occupancy has steadily improved since April, rising from 53.3% to 56.0% on a square-meter basis and from 37.0% to 37.9% on a unit basis. Rental velocity has turned positive with net gains of 2 units in May and 6 units in June, driven by consistently strong move-ins outpacing vacates. Revenue grew 5.3% over the period to €35,939.69 in June, though the average rate per square meter declined slightly from €11.54 to €11.23, suggesting the gains came from volume rather than pricing. Arrears have worsened materially, climbing from €3,700.70 with 45 accounts in arrears to €7,987.43 with 59 accounts by June, which warrants closer attention.
