Occupancy by unit count declined slightly from 37.5% in March to 37.0% in April before recovering to 37.2% in May, while square-meter occupancy remained relatively flat across the three-month span (54.5% to 54.8%). Rental velocity weakened significantly, with the site shifting from net +19 units in March to net -3 units in April, then recovering modestly to net +2 units in May. Revenue remained relatively stable month-to-month (€30,309 to €34,801), though the rate per square meter declined slightly from €11.72 to €11.54. Arrears are a growing concern, rising substantially from €2,403 across 41 accounts in March to €6,723 across 46 accounts by May.
