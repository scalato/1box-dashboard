1BOX Helmond Kanaaldijk has shown strong momentum on occupancy and unit acquisition since January, with occupancy rising from 32.7% to 37.5% by unit count and 48.3% to 54.5% by square meter, while netting 29 new rentals across the three-month span (February saw +10 units, March saw +19). Rental velocity accelerated sharply in March with 39 move-ins, though revenue declined in March to €30,309 despite higher occupancy, driven by a drop in average rate per square meter from €12.34 to €11.72. Arrears spiked to €36,693 in February before falling to €2,403 in March, though the count of accounts in arrears rose to 41, suggesting recent delinquencies among newer tenants rather than persistent bad debt.
