Occupancy at 1BOX Breda has declined modestly from 56.5% to 55.3% in unit terms since January, with a sharp downturn in February when the site lost 5 net units, though March stabilized with near-flat activity (net +1 unit). Rental velocity remains weak—February saw only 21 move-ins against 26 vacates, and while March recovered to 37 move-ins, demand remains below January's level. Revenue has edged down 1.4% across the period despite a slight uptick in rate per sqm from 13.94 to 14.01, suggesting pricing adjustments have partially offset occupancy loss. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to 42,446 euros across 94 accounts before improving sharply in March to 10,741 euros, indicating either collection activity or a single large payment rather than a sustained delinquency trend.
