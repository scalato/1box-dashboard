Occupancy at 1BOX Breda improved significantly in May, rising to 57.9% on a unit basis (up 2.3 points from March) and 60.0% on a square-meter basis, driven by strong net rentals of 17 units that month compared to only 1 net unit in March. Revenue remained relatively flat at approximately €46,900 monthly despite the occupancy gains, as the average rate per sqm declined from €14.01 in March to €13.02 in May, suggesting a mix shift toward lower-priced units. Arrears improved notably, dropping from €10,741 outstanding in March to €7,225 in May, though the count of accounts in arrears edged up to 74, indicating better overall collection on existing balances.
