Occupancy at 1BOX Breda improved steadily over the three-month span, rising from 55.6% to 60.3% by unit count and from 57.4% to 60.0% by square meter, driven by strong net rental activity of 3, 17, and 19 units in April, May, and June respectively. Revenue climbed from €45,874 to €47,675 month-over-month, while the average rate per square meter stabilized around €13.02–€13.50 across the period. Arrears outstanding fluctuated between €7,225 and €9,907, though arrears count dropped significantly to 19 accounts in June, suggesting improved collection or tenant quality.
