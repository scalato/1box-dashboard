1BOX Alkmaar showed mixed performance across the three-month window. Occupancy by unit count declined from 81.4% in April to 80.2% in June, with net rentals turning negative in both April (-5 units) and June (-7 units), though May posted a modest gain of +2 units. Revenue remained relatively flat around €49,750–€50,130 monthly, while rent-per-sqm improved from €19.26 to €19.86. Arrears outstanding held steady near €24,500 across the period, though the count of accounts in arrears dropped from 32 to 23 in June, suggesting some collection activity.
