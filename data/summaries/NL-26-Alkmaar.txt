Occupancy by square meter recovered in May to 86.2% after declining to 84.8% in April, though unit-level occupancy remained relatively flat between 81.4% and 81.6% across the three months. Rental velocity turned positive in May with a net gain of 2 units, reversing two consecutive months of losses (net -6 in March, net -5 in April). Revenue grew incrementally from €49,150 in March to €50,132 in May, with average rate per square meter rising from 19.2 to 19.52 over the span. Arrears outstanding increased slightly to €24,779 by May from €23,836 in March, with arrears count holding steady at 32 in both April and May.
