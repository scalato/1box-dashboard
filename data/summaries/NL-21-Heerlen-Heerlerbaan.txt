Occupancy improved modestly across the three-month window, rising from 47.5% to 50.5% by unit count, though square-metre occupancy peaked in May at 70.9% before slipping slightly to 70.2% in June. Net rental activity remained positive but decelerating, with 9 net units gained in May followed by only 3 in June, as move-in velocity slowed from 16 to 12 units monthly. Revenue grew steadily from €22,074 to €23,136, driven partly by rate increases (€11.00 to €11.62 per sqm), while arrears improved in count (39 to 16 accounts) but spiked again in outstanding balance to €3,549 by June. The site shows modest occupancy growth and pricing strength, though June's slower leasing momentum and rising arrears balances warrant monitoring.
