Occupancy at 1BOX Heerlen-Heerlerbaan improved meaningfully over the three-month span, rising from 47.0% to 49.7% by unit count and 68.3% to 70.9% by square meter, with May showing the strongest performance at +9 net rentals. Rental velocity accelerated notably: after a soft March with net -4 units, the site gained traction in April (+2 units) and delivered a strong May (+9 units) driven by 16 move-ins against only 7 vacates. Revenue grew modestly from €21,970 to €22,393, while the per-square-meter rate held steady around €11. Arrears improved in May, declining to €2,545 across 20 accounts from a peak of €3,182 in April, suggesting better payment compliance as occupancy rose.
