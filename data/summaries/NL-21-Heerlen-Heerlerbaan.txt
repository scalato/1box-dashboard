Occupancy by square meter has improved modestly since January, rising from 62.3% to 68.3%, though unit-level occupancy has remained flat around 45-48% despite net rental activity swinging between gains and losses. Rental velocity has been volatile, with February showing strong net gains of 10 units but March reversing to net losses of 4 units as vacates picked up. Revenue has grown steadily month-over-month from €20,277 to €21,970, supported by rising average rates per square meter (€10.96 to €11.12), indicating effective pricing. A notable spike in arrears outstanding appeared in February (€19,231) but has since fallen sharply to €2,025 in March, though the arrears account count has risen to 46 accounts, suggesting some resolution of larger debts but persistent collection challenges across more tenants.
