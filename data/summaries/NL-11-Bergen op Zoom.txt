1BOX Bergen op Zoom has shown declining momentum since January. Occupancy by unit count fell from 65.9% in January to 64.5% in March, with net rentals turning negative in March (minus 5 units) after a marginal gain of 1 unit in February. Revenue declined 0.9% over the three-month span to €21,381.58, and rate per sqm dipped slightly from €17.25 to €17.18. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to €17,940 across 13 accounts before falling back, though arrears count continued climbing to 20 accounts by March, signaling emerging collection issues.
