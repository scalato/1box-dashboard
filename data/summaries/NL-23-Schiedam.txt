1BOX Schiedam experienced deteriorating performance in June after modest gains in May. Unit occupancy climbed from 83.0% in April to 83.9% in May, then dropped sharply to 80.8% in June with a net loss of 18 units driven by 29 vacates against just 9 move-ins. Square-meter occupancy followed a similar pattern, peaking at 91.1% in May before declining to 88.9% in June. Revenue remained relatively flat across the three months (ranging from €75,040 to €76,208), while the site's arrears position improved notably, falling from €17,959 across 70 accounts in April to €15,702 across just 26 accounts by June.
