Occupancy at 1BOX Schiedam has climbed steadily over the three-month period, rising from 81.6% to 83.9% by unit count and from 87.6% to 91.1% by square meter, driven by net positive rentals each month though velocity has slowed (net +12 units in March, declining to +5 in May). Revenue grew modestly from €74,439 to €76,208, while average rate per square meter dipped slightly to €24.20 in May after peaking in March. Arrears increased sharply in April to €17,959 across 70 accounts but have since stabilized near that level in May, warranting monitoring.
