Occupancy by unit count declined from 81.0% in January to 79.8% in February before recovering to 81.6% in March, with net rentals swinging from negative (−7 units in February) to strongly positive (+12 units in March). Square-meter occupancy shows a clearer upward trend, rising from 84.3% in January to 87.6% by March. Revenue remained relatively flat across the period (€75,120 to €74,439), while the rate per sqm fell from €25.61 in February to €24.41 in March, and arrears outstanding dropped significantly from €69,492 in February to €11,649 in March.
