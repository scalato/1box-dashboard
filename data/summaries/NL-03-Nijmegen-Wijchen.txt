Occupancy by unit count grew from 81.0% in April to 83.6% in June, gaining 7 units net over the two-month span, though momentum stalled in June with zero net rentals. Square-meter occupancy peaked at 87.0% in May before declining to 85.0% in June, signaling a slight softening in space utilization. Revenue remained stable around €32,000 monthly despite the June occupancy dip, supported by a rising average rate per square meter that increased from €20.18 to €20.83. Arrears nearly doubled from €2,716 in April to €5,668 in June, a notable flag despite a lower count of delinquent accounts, suggesting concentration among fewer but larger outstanding balances.
