Occupancy by square meter peaked in February at 86.6% before declining to 85.0% in March, while unit-level occupancy has remained relatively flat between 77–80% across the three-month span. Rental velocity has been inconsistent, swinging from a net loss of 10 units in January, a gain of 7 in February, to a net loss of 3 in March, suggesting unstable leasing momentum. Revenue has remained stable at roughly €31,000 monthly despite the occupancy fluctuations and a declining average rate per square meter (down from €20.65 to €19.84). Arrears spiked sharply in February to €29,867 but fell dramatically to €4,924 by March, though the arrears count increased to 25 accounts, indicating collection volatility rather than a systemic improvement.
