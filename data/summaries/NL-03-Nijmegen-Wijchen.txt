Occupancy has risen steadily across the three-month window, with unit occupancy climbing from 78.6% in March to 83.0% in May, driven by positive net rentals of 9 units in April and 6 units in May following a 3-unit loss in March. Revenue has remained stable around €31,500–€32,000 monthly despite the occupancy gains, while the average rate per square meter increased modestly from €19.84 to €20.33. Arrears have improved notably, declining from €4,924 across 25 accounts in March to €2,863 across 21 accounts by May, indicating better collection performance alongside the operational gains.
