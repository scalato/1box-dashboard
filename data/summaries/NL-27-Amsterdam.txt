Occupancy has trended upward over the three-month period, with unit occupancy rising from 42.8% in March to 44.0% in May and square-meter occupancy climbing from 37.0% to 40.4%. Net rentals turned positive in April with 8 new units added, though momentum slowed in May with only 2 net additions. Revenue grew steadily from €34,347 in March to €37,814 in May, supported by rate increases from €13.87/sqm to €14.56/sqm. Arrears are a concern, nearly tripling from €4,402 in March to €9,922 in May despite a fluctuation in May (arrears count rose to 53 accounts), warranting closer collection attention.
