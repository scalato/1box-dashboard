Occupancy at Amsterdam-Schepenbergweg has declined modestly since January, falling from 43.6% to 42.8% by unit count and 38.3% to 37.0% by square meter, driven by negative net rentals in February and March (minus 3 units each month) as vacates have outpaced move-ins. Revenue has remained relatively flat around €34,000 monthly despite the occupancy slip, supported by a rising average rate per square meter that climbed from €13.15 to €13.87. Arrears spiked to €30,559 in February—a six-fold increase from January—before normalizing back to €4,402 in March, though the arrears count continued to climb to 51 accounts, indicating potential collection headwinds.
