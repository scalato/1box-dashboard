1BOX Roermond has experienced a sharp occupancy decline over the three-month period, dropping from 91.8% unit occupancy in March to 85.8% in May, driven by weak rental activity—the site lost 11 net units in May alone after losing 2 in April. Revenue remained relatively stable around €21,000–€22,000 despite the occupancy slide, with per-sqm rates holding in the €16.67–€17.38 range. Arrears improved notably, falling from €3,945 outstanding across 17 accounts in March to €2,526 across 13 accounts by May, suggesting better collection discipline.
