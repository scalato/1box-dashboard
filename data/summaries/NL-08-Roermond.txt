Occupancy at 1BOX Roermond has declined steadily over the three-month period, falling from 90.7% unit occupancy in April to 83.6% in June, a loss of 7.1 percentage points driven by persistent negative rental velocity—the site shed 15 net units across the span (net -2, -9, and -4 units month-over-month). Revenue has remained relatively flat despite the occupancy decline, hovering between €21,300 and €22,050, supported by incremental rate increases from €16.67 to €17.71 per sqm. Arrears have nearly doubled from €2,014 across 12 accounts in April to €4,809 across 14 accounts in June, signaling deteriorating collection performance alongside the softening occupancy trend.
