Occupancy has declined steadily over the three-month period, with unit occupancy falling from 77.5% in March to 76.2% in May, while square-meter occupancy remained flat at approximately 79.5%. Rental velocity has been consistently negative, with net losses of 2 units in March and April and 3 units in May, driven by higher vacates than move-ins each month. Revenue has remained stable around €48,500 monthly despite the occupancy decline, supported by slight rate increases from €20.00 to €20.06 per square meter. Arrears spiked to €3,503 outstanding in May (18 accounts) after improving in April, indicating potential collection risk that warrants monitoring.
