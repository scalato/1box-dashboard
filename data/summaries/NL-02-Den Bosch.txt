Occupancy declined modestly from 82.7% to 79.5% by square meter since January, with unit occupancy holding relatively flat around 77-78%. Rental velocity has been weak, oscillating between net losses and minimal gains—the site lost 12 units in January, gained 3 in February, then lost 2 in March—suggesting difficulty sustaining tenant growth. Revenue remained stable around 49,000 euros monthly despite the occupancy slip, supported by a slight rise in average rent per square meter from 19.83 to 20.0. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to 48,551 euros before falling back sharply in March to 3,369 euros, indicating a collection issue that was largely resolved.
