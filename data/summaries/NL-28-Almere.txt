Occupancy at 1BOX Almere has climbed steadily from 43.4% unit occupancy in January to 46.6% in March, with sqm occupancy rising 3.6 percentage points across the same period; net rentals averaged 5 units per month despite consistent move-in activity of 12 units, indicating moderate but steady growth. Revenue increased 4.2% from January to March (€15,636 to €16,289), driven by both occupancy gains and a modest rate increase to €10.87 per sqm in March. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to €15,128 before normalizing to €1,573 in March, though the underlying count remained stable at 14 accounts in arrears.
