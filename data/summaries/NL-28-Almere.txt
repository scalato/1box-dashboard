1BOX Almere remained broadly flat over the April–June period, with occupancy hovering around 47% by unit count (46.6% to 46.9%) and sqm occupancy between 61.4% and 62.1%. Net rental activity was minimal, with only a single net addition in May while April and June each saw zero net movement despite healthy move-in traffic. Revenue improved modestly from €17,101 in April to €17,475 in June, driven largely by a rate increase from €10.82 to €11.44 per sqm rather than volume growth. Arrears declined significantly from €4,177 to €2,818, though the count of accounts in arrears remained elevated at 21 as of June.
