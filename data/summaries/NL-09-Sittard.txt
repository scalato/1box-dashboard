1BOX Sittard showed modest occupancy gains since January, with square-meter occupancy rising from 76.9% to 77.4% while unit occupancy held relatively flat around 67–68%. Net rental activity was minimal across the period, with a single month of +5 net units in February offset by zero net movement in both January and March. Revenue remained stable at roughly €31,500–€32,500 per month with no meaningful rate pressure, though arrears spiked sharply in February to €29,674 before falling back to €2,865 in March, suggesting a collection anomaly or recovery rather than a trend. Overall the site is holding steady without meaningful growth or deterioration.
