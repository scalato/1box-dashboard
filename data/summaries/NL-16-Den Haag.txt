Occupancy has declined modestly from 88.6% to 86.7% by square metre over the three-month span, with unit-level occupancy slipping from 84.8% to 84.0% in May. Rental velocity has been weak, with net losses of six units in March and five units in May, offset only by a marginal gain of two units in April. Revenue per square metre improved to €23.25 in May from €22.55 in March despite the occupancy decline, while overall net revenue remained stable around €73,000–€75,600 monthly. Arrears outstanding increased to €14,569 by May, though the count of accounts in arrears fell from 108 to 66, suggesting concentration among fewer tenants.
