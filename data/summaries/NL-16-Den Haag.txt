Occupancy has declined modestly over the three-month period, dropping from 84.8% to 83.3% by unit count, driven by negative net rentals of -5 units in May and -4 units in June despite higher move-in activity in June. Revenue has grown incrementally from €73,276 to €77,108, with the average rate per square meter increasing 4.4% from €22.61 to €23.61, offsetting the occupancy loss. Arrears outstanding have surged significantly to €20,567 in June from €13,946 in April, though the count of delinquent accounts has declined, suggesting concentration among fewer tenants with larger balances.
