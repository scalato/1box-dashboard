Occupancy at 1BOX Den Haag has declined steadily since January, dropping 1.8 percentage points by unit count (from 87.7% to 84.8%) and 1.8 points by square meter (from 90.4% to 88.6%), driven by persistently negative net rentals of minus 14 units in February and minus 6 units in March. Move-ins have softened from 33 in January to an average of 26 in the following two months, while vacates have remained elevated at 36 units per month. Revenue has held relatively stable around 73,500 to 74,500 despite the occupancy pressure, supported by modest rate growth to 22.55 per square meter in March. Arrears spiked sharply in February to 64,212 outstanding but recovered to 13,583 by March, suggesting a collection effort or tenant departure.
