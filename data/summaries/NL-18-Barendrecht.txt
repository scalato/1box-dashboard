1BOX Barendrecht reversed a two-month occupancy decline in May, with unit occupancy rising to 78.0% (up 1.1 points from April) after dropping 1.8 points from March to April. Rental velocity turned sharply positive in May with net +20 units, a significant swing from net -10 in April and net -7 in March, driven by a spike in move-ins to 31 units against only 11 vacates. Revenue recovered to €63,063.68 in May, the highest of the three months, supported by improved occupancy and a notable increase in average rate per sqm to €18.49. Arrears outstanding rose to €6,191.98 in May despite a slight improvement in arrears count, warranting attention as the largest monthly amount in the window.
