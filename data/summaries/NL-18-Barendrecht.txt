Occupancy at 1BOX Barendrecht has declined steadily from January through March, with unit occupancy falling 3.5 percentage points (80.2% to 76.7%) and square-meter occupancy dropping 5.2 points (86.5% to 81.3%). Rental velocity has been negative both months, with net losses of 14 units in February and 7 units in March as vacates have consistently exceeded move-ins. Revenue remained relatively flat around 63,000 euros despite the occupancy slide, supported by rising rates per square meter that increased from 17.11 to 17.71. Arrears spiked sharply in February to over 61,000 euros before improving substantially in March to 4,770 euros, suggesting a collection recovery.
