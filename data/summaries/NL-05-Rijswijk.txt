Occupancy declined steadily from January through March, dropping 1.2 percentage points by unit count (84.2% to 83.0%) as the site lost net 12 units over the period, driven by vacates exceeding move-ins in both January and March. Revenue remained relatively flat around €67k monthly despite the occupancy slip, supported by a modest rate increase to €25.57 per sqm in March. Arrears spiked sharply in February to €65.6k across 81 accounts before improving substantially in March to €4.2k across 56 accounts, suggesting either collection activity or a one-time payment event.
