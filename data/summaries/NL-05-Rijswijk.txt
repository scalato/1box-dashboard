1BOX Rijswijk showed steady occupancy growth over the three-month period, with square-meter occupancy rising from 85.2% in March to 88.2% in May, driven by consistent positive net rentals of 6 and 8 units in April and May respectively after a loss of 7 units in March. Revenue climbed incrementally from €67,550 to €69,994, while average rate per square meter held stable around €25.56–25.71. Arrears have become a concern, growing substantially from €4,249 outstanding across 56 accounts in March to €10,047 across 97 accounts by May, warranting attention to collection processes.
