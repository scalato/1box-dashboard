Occupancy at 1BOX Rijswijk declined in June after a brief improvement, dropping 2.4 percentage points on a unit basis from May's peak of 84.5% to 82.1%, driven by a sharp spike in vacates (35 units versus 7 in May) that outpaced move-ins. Net rentals turned negative in June with a loss of 16 units, reversing the modest gains of April and May when the site added 6 and 8 units respectively. Revenue remained relatively flat across the three-month window at approximately €68,300–€70,000 monthly despite the occupancy swings, though per-square-meter rates edged up slightly to €25.82. Arrears improved notably in June, falling to €6,320 across 50 accounts from a peak of €10,047 in May, suggesting recent collection activity.
