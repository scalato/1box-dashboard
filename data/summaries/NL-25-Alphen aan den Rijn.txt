1BOX Alphen aan den Rijn has experienced a steady occupancy decline since January, with unit occupancy falling from 90.9% to 86.1% over three months as vacates have consistently outpaced move-ins, resulting in net losses of 12 units in February and 11 units in March. Revenue has declined from €59,947 in January to €58,391 in March, though the average rate per square meter has inched upward from €21.06 to €21.50, suggesting pricing strength despite volume pressure. Arrears spiked dramatically in February to €54,538 before normalizing to €1,394 in March, indicating a collection recovery. The site is losing rental momentum with persistent negative net rentals and needs to reverse the vacancy trend to stabilize performance.
