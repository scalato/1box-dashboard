Occupancy at 1BOX Helmond has declined steadily since January, with unit occupancy falling from 82.9% to 78.4% and square-meter occupancy dropping from 85.9% to 82.2% over the three-month period. Net rentals have turned sharply negative, with the site losing 4 units in February and 9 units in March, driven by vacates consistently outpacing move-ins. Revenue remained relatively flat around €32,200–€32,500 despite occupancy headwinds, as the average rate per square meter edged up slightly to €19.40 by March. Arrears present a mixed picture: outstanding balance spiked to €32,460 in February before falling back to €4,367 in March, though the number of accounts in arrears increased from 26 to 43 over the span.
