1BOX Helmond has experienced a sharp occupancy decline over the three-month period, with unit occupancy falling from 76.4% in April to 71.9% in June, representing a loss of 13 rented units and a net-3 to net-11 rental swing across the months. Revenue dropped 3.9% from April to June despite a slight uptick in rate-per-sqm, declining from €31,127 to €29,917 as occupancy erosion outweighed pricing gains. June was particularly weak with 16 vacates against only 3 move-ins, driving the net-11 rental loss; however, arrears improved meaningfully, dropping from 43 accounts and €4,237 outstanding in April to 22 accounts and €3,790 by June.
