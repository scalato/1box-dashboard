Occupancy at 1BOX Helmond declined steadily over the three-month window, with unit occupancy dropping from 78.4% in March to 76.4% in April and holding flat through May, while square-meter occupancy fell 3.3 points from 82.2% to 78.9%. Rental velocity improved markedly, moving from a net loss of 9 units in March to a net loss of only 3 units in April, and then turning positive with a net gain of 2 units in May, driven by 11 move-ins against 9 vacates. Revenue declined 4.2% over the span despite a slight uptick in average rate per square meter from €19.40 to €19.83, suggesting the occupancy losses outweighed rate gains. Arrears are trending favorably, with outstanding amount down 4.2% and tenant count reduced from 43 to 38, indicating improving collection discipline.
