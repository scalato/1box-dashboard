1BOX Colomiers has remained relatively flat across the three-month period, with occupancy hovering between 61.5% and 62.1% by unit count and declining slightly by square meter from 64.9% to 64.4%. Rental velocity has weakened, with net rentals dropping from +5 units in March to breakeven by May as move-ins have fallen from 24 to 19 per month. Revenue recovered in May to €34,347, rebounding from April's dip, driven partly by rate increases (€12.12 to €12.29 per sqm), though arrears have risen notably from €42,428 to €45,097 with tenant count in arrears increasing from 78 to 88.
