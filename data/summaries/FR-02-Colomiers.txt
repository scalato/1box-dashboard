Occupancy has improved steadily over the three-month span, rising from 61.5% to 62.8% by unit count and from 63.6% to 66.0% by square metre, driven primarily by strong net rentals of 8 units in June after a flat May. Revenue remains relatively stable between €32k and €34k per month, though the rate per square metre has held firm around €12.20–€12.29, indicating consistent pricing discipline. Rental velocity turned positive in June with 25 move-ins against 17 vacates, reversing the slight losses seen in April and May. Arrears rose noticeably from €41.7k in April to €45.1k in May before moderating to €44.3k in June, with the account count fluctuating between 68 and 88 cases—a flag worth monitoring.
