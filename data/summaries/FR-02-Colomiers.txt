Occupancy has remained flat across the three-month span, hovering between 61–63% by unit count with a slight recovery in March to 62.1%. Rental velocity has been weak, with net gains of only 7 units in January followed by a loss of 7 units in February and a modest recovery of 5 units in March, indicating unstable tenant demand. Revenue per square meter has grown steadily from 11.1 to 12.12 since January, driven by rate increases rather than volume, while net revenue rose from 30,879 to 33,319 over the period. Arrears are a growing concern, increasing from 38,456 to 42,428 outstanding and climbing from 67 to 78 accounts in arrears, signaling deteriorating collections.
