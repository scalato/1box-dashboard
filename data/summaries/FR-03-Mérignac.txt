1BOX Mérignac showed steady occupancy growth over the three-month period, with unit occupancy rising from 46.0% in April to 50.5% in June (up 4.5 points), while square-meter occupancy climbed 2.2 points to 54.0%. Rental velocity accelerated sharply, moving from a net gain of just 1 unit in April to net +11 units in May and +19 units in June, driven by increasing move-ins (21 to 34) and declining vacates. Revenue grew 3.5% from April to June (€36,285 to €37,541), with average rate per square meter improving from 11.6 to 12.03. Arrears remain a concern, rising from €38,432 (13 accounts) in April to €43,597 (23 accounts) in June, indicating growing collection risk despite operational momentum.
