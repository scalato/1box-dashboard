Occupancy by unit count improved from 45.8% in March to 47.7% in May, a gain of 12 rented units driven by net additions of 16 in March and 11 in May, though April saw minimal net growth with only 1 new rental. Revenue remained relatively stable at €33–36k monthly, with the per-square-meter rate edging up from €11.53 to €11.86 over the period. Arrears outstanding held steady around €38–39k, though the count of accounts in arrears dropped significantly from 43 in March to 17 in May, suggesting improved collections or resolution of some delinquencies.
